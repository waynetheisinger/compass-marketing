# MowDirect SEO Dashboard — Shopify Reports to Build

All reports filtered to **organic search traffic** as the source. Built inside Shopify Analytics, linked from a weekly Shopify Flow email sent Monday mornings.

## Headlines (summary KPIs, period-over-period)

1. **Organic Revenue** — month-on-month and year-on-year
2. **Organic Orders** — count, with comparisons
3. **Organic Conversion Rate** — target +0.5pp
4. **Organic AOV** — compare against other channels

## Trends (line charts over time)

5. **Organic Revenue over time** — monthly, with seasonal baseline overlay
6. **Organic Sessions over time** — monthly, north star +40% YoY

## What's Selling from Search

7. **Top 10 products by organic revenue** — product name, revenue, units
8. **Top 10 landing pages by organic sessions** — validates content calendar
9. **Product page views from organic** — eyeballs vs wallets

## Funnel Health

10. **Add-to-cart rate (organic)** — early funnel indicator
11. **Cart abandonment rate (organic)** — target -15%
12. **New vs returning customers (organic)** — loyalty check
13. **Returning customer rate (organic) over time** — compounding indicator

## Revenue Quality

14. **Sales by product (organic)** — full hero product calendar validation
15. **Revenue by landing page (organic)** — pairs with report #8
16. **Orders by landing page (organic)** — volume vs value split

## Delivery

- **Shopify Flow** weekly scheduled email, Monday mornings
- Email contains grouped links to each report above (URLs preserve filters when shared)
- Recipients need staff accounts with Reports permission

## Excluded (require external tools, not in scope)

Keyword rankings, backlinks, Core Web Vitals, crawl errors, SERP CTR, competitor benchmarks, content gap analysis — handled separately via Google Search Console, Ahrefs, and SimilarWeb.
